Welcome!

If you do not know what airsoft is I will briefly explain it, if you do, then skip this paragraph. Airsoft is a sport (if you can call it that) where you take plastic guns with orange muzzles and shoot people with 6mm bbs at around 400FPS. Airsoft is an all-inclusive sport/game.

This "game" is a mash-up of airsoft and the game known as Skyrim. I created it out of the desire to play more airsoft with my friends and family without getting burnt out. You may be wondering: "What do I do in this game?" In this group you can: Buy weapons, raid enemy stashes, team up, scout out enemy groups, rule the land with an iron fist, get rich, and many more things if you let me explain (on the game page).

Our Airsoft Rules

We play by general airsoft rules: one-hit eliminations (unless otherwise stated), no shooting someone at less than 15ft, no guns higher than 420FPS (400FPS is recommended), you can get hit while reloading, no full-auto, and no blind firing.
